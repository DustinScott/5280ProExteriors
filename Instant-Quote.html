Pricing Factors

What Impacts the Cost of a Roof Replacement H2

Roofing costs vary depending on several important factors.

Roof Size H3

Larger roofs require more materials and labor, directly affecting project cost.

Roof Pitch & Complexity H3

Steeper roofs or those with valleys, dormers, and architectural features require more time and specialized methods.

Roofing Material H3

Different systems have different price ranges depending on durability, lifespan, and installation requirements.

Existing Roof Layers H3

Multiple existing layers may require additional tear-off work before new materials can be installed.

Roof Deck Condition H3

Damaged decking may need repair or replacement before new roofing materials are applied.

Know the Difference

Online Estimates vs. Final Roofing Quotes H2

An instant online estimate provides a helpful starting point — but it is not a final quote.

Online Estimate

  • Instant price range in seconds
  • Based on aerial EagleView measurements
  • No appointment needed
  • Helps with budgeting & planning
  • Free, no commitment required

Final Roofing Quote

Requires professional inspection. Final price depends on:

  • Exact roof measurements
  • Roof decking condition
  • Ventilation improvements
  • Flashing replacement
  • Underlayment requirements
  • Storm damage repairs

After reviewing your estimate, the next step is a professional roof inspection to confirm the final scope of work.
